About Sun Happy Feet

Sun Happy Feet, Get Relaxed. Raising the standard for massage therapy in North Dallas.

"We define excellence by providing unparalleled personal care service in an environment filled with a caring and dedicated team."

Above all else, was created as a place of well-being. By catering to the mind, body and soul, we have created a retreat from the everyday – a haven of pure relaxation. Sun Happy Feet invites you to experience every aspect of our unique environment and the services we offer. Our professional therapists listen to you, and customize massage therapy to ease your muscle issues. Whether you are simply looking for stress relief, seeking an escape from the trappings of modern life, experiencing discomfort from your workout or pain associated with an injury, Sun Happy Feet has a massage treatment that ideally fits your particular condition.

As to what sets us apart and what we are most proud of, it would be that a high percentage of our business is from repeat customers and referrals. Happy satisfied customers not only return to us but spread the word about all that we have to offer. Also, we pride ourselves on our integrity and in case your visit is ever less than satisfactory, we need to know about it so that we can correct the problem. Each and every client at the spa is important to us and we want your experience to be the very best.
